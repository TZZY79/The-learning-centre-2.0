Vocabulary: Salon & Spa

üìö Students Read:

  • To get a trim - to cut only the ends of the hair.
  • Highlights - coloring small sections of hair lighter.
  • A Facial - a deep treatment for your face skin.
  • Manicure/Pedicure - cleaning and styling your nails.

Interesting Hairstyles

üìö Students Read:

  • A Bob - hair cut straight around the jawline.
  • A Buzz Cut - very short hair cut with clippers.
  • Bangs (Fringe) - hair covering the forehead.
  • Layers - hair cut at different lengths.

Grammar: If & Whether

üìö Students Read:

We use if and whether to talk about choices or "yes/no" ideas inside a sentence.

"I don't know if I want a trim."

"She's deciding whether to get highlights or not."

Whether vs If

üìö Students Read:

1. They are mostly the same!

2. Whether is more formal.

3. Use Whether before "or not".

"I don't know whether or not layers look good on me."
